Privacy Policy

XP3R QBO MCP · Last updated: 13 June 2026

XP3R QBO MCP ("the App") is a private, internal tool operated by XP3R Inc ("we", "us"). It is used exclusively by XP3R Inc to access its own QuickBooks Online company data. The App is not distributed to, or used by, third parties.

Information we access

With your authorization through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow, the App accesses QuickBooks Online accounting data belonging to XP3R Inc — including the chart of accounts, transactions, and financial reports — on a read-only basis. The App does not create, modify, or delete any QuickBooks data.

How we use and store information

  • Accounting data is retrieved on demand and processed locally to support internal bookkeeping review and analysis. It is not retained in a separate datastore by the App.
  • OAuth tokens (used to authenticate to Intuit on your behalf) are stored locally on the operator's machine in the operating system's secure credential store. They are never committed to source control or transmitted to us.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any third party. Data is not used for advertising or profiling.

Data sharing

The only external service the App communicates with is Intuit's QuickBooks Online API, for the sole purpose of retrieving XP3R Inc's own data. No data is sent to any other third party.

Data retention and deletion

Because data is processed locally and on demand, you can revoke the App's access at any time from your Intuit account, and delete the locally stored tokens, which immediately ends all access.
